Other Places & Groups that are Known to be Safe for Trans Folk

Please Note: These groups are NOT trans-only but will ALWAYS be trans-friendly.

  • Five Rings Fencing Club

    Ran by Mark Wickersham who has over 20 years of professional and historical fencing and strongly believes in creating safe spaces for trans althletes to grow.

  • Choral Spectrum

    A mixed chorus (SATB) for the LGBTQIA and Supporting community.

  • Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence

    The local chapter of the Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence who are supportive and organizing members during Trans Day of Remmberance Events

  • Boffer(foam sword battle reenactment, beginner friendly -no equipment needed)
