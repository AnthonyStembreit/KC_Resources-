KC Trans Resources

Support Groups & Community Centers

  • KC Center for Inclusion

    Hosts Equal Trans Support Group, has free binder give away, and clothing exchange.

  • Transformations KC

    Trans Femme BIPOC specific, offers mentorship programs and yearly retreat to Ozarks.

Local Media

  • Trans Talk

    Trans Talk, monthly radio show with historical podcast.

Other Places & Groups that are Known to be Safe for Trans Folk

Note: These groups are NOT trans-only but will ALWAYS be trans-friendly. Additionally, please contact me if you have a place to add to this list.

  • Five Rings Fencing Club

    Ran by Mark Wickersham who has over 20 years of professional and historical fencing and strongly believes in creating safe spaces for trans althletes to grow.

  • Choral Spectrum

    A mixed chorus (SATB) for the LGBTQIA and Supporting community.

  • Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence

    The local chapter of the Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence who are supportive and organizing members during Trans Day of Remmberance Events.

  • Boffer(foam sword battle re-enactment, beginner friendly / no equipment needed)
    Meet-up Tuesdays @6pm Thomas Park 12500 W 119th St, Overland Park, KS 66210
    Ran by Quinn, a local non-binary community member.

Healthcare

  • KC Care

    Can offer Trans healthcare to the uninsured KC community for low-cost.

  • Truman / University Health

    Offers FTM and MTF top surgery.

  • KU Med

    Offers gender affirming medicine including top and bottom surgeries for FTM and MTF, see website for more details.

Housing

  • Lion House

    Transitional housing for Homeless LGBT+ teens, some housing is available 18-24 as well.
